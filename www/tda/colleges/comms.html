Castle

Welcome to Castle House. We are home to approximately 360 students and 30 teaching staff. Our aim is to provide exciting and challenging learning experiences that offer both creative and academic challenge and equip our young people to be confident communicators and users of language.

Our College
The Arts College has a curriculum and pastoral role. The Arts team are pastoral and academic tutors to 350 TDA students.
College Leader
My name is Emma Taplin and I am extremely proud to be leading the Communication College; the team and myself are committed to providing the very best care for students and we look forward to welcoming you to join our community.
College Captains
Four College Captains make up the student leadership team, they are Jade Moretti, Carley-Jane Beech, Dani Gordon and Masooma Malik. Their roles are highly valued and play a central role in helping our young people share in the decision making about their school.
