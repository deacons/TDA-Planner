Highfield

Welcome to Highfield. The Highfield team is made up of 25 members of teaching staff, 3 teaching assistants and 2 administrators. We offer a wide range of subjects across the year groups consisting of Geography, History, Religious Studies, Business Studies, Economics, Politics, Psychology and Sociology.

Our College
Humanities is one of the larger colleges with over 350 students. We have a fantastic pastoral team who work alongside students to help them excel in every aspect of their education.
